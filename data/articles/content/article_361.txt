Troubleshooting ¶ For more information regarding common troubleshooting problems and solutions, check out DualShock 4 Troubleshooting Background ¶ The Sony Model CUH-ZCT1U Wireless Controller, also known as the Dualshock 4 was released at the end of 2013 to replace the previous six-axis controller. The Dualshock 4 is compatible with the Playstation 4 and Playstation 3. Although compatibility with the Playstation 3 is limited to newer games, the Dualshock 4 will work while it is connected with a USB to Micro B USB cable. The Dualshock 4 also includes several new features such as a headphone jack, a built-in Mono stereo, and a two point touchpad.
